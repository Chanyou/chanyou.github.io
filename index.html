Carpool Network

Carpool Network is a novel route-based carpool matching system.
In those days(2011), carpool matching services were region-based. They just matched the people who have similar destination and departure point simply. This approach makes some burden for users. They need to estimate the amount of detour distance when they are inviting or joining in a carpool. Also, it makes them to miss the chance to carpool in the middle of a route. To remedy this, we developed a system, Carpool Network.
